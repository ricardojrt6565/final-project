How to be a successful student in SEP?

  1. Be interested in what you are learning
    • If you are not interested in what you are learning then youn will not focus or work as hard as you should be
    • It's important to like what you are learning, because there are alot of projects that you will have to do
  2. Be creative
    • Look for inspration all around you
    • Look up examples for your projects
    • Share your ideas with your teacher and partner
    • Create a plan to make your ideas reality
  3. Help others
    • Do your best to help anyone you can
    • Share your ideas to inspire others
    • When you help others you better understand the things you learned
    • If you help others then others will be glad to help you
