How to be a successful student in SEP?

Be Interested in what you're learning

  • If not you're not going to be learning or working as hard as you should be!
  • it's important to like what you're doing because there are a ton of projects ahead.

    • Be Creative

  • Inspiration is all around you! You just have to be seeking the knowledge.
  • Look at examples, they help so much!
  • Share your ideas, don't be SHY the worst that can happen is that no one will hear your ideas, so share it!

    • Create a plan to make your ideas reality

  • Help others!
  • Do your best by helping as much as you can!
  • Share your ideas to inspire others.
  • When you help others you better understand the things you are learning.
  • Your classmates are not only there to slow you down, they also can come in handy by helping you!
