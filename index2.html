How to be a successful student in SEP?

Be Interested in what you're learning

  • If you're not interested in what you're learning, then you will find the class boring and difficult.
  • It's important to like what you're doing because you can create some really amazing projects.
  • You will have a greater time in class if you like learning about soft ware.
  • There are endless, fun & amazing topics to learn about like webdesign, robots, apps and so much more!

    • Be Creative

  • Inspiration is all around you! You just have to look around you and look at examples from class or online.
  • Look at examples, they help so much!
  • Share your ideas with the class. You can be your own inspiration and be an inspiration for others if you share your ideas.

    • Create a plan to make your ideas a reality

  • Helping others will give you a better understanding of what you learned in class.
  • Do your best to help others as much as you can, because they will help you in return.
  • Before you can create something you must envision it.
  • Decide how you are goning to do something. And then create a step by step plan on google drawings or a piece of paper.
  • Ask for help if you are stuck. You teacher or classmates will be happy to help you.
